1. 🛬 Reception & Registration (15 min)

  • Welcome table & sign-in
  • Light refreshments and informal networking
  • Distribution of printed agenda & member list

2. 🎙️ Opening Remarks (10 min)

  • Welcome by Chairperson
  • Purpose of the meeting
  • Ground rules for participation

3. 👥 Introduction Round (15–20 min)

  • All attendees introduce themselves
  • Highlight new members and their background

4. 📜 Presentation of Vision & Mission (10–15 min)

  • Introduction to NRNA Belgium’s vision
  • Explanation of mission and values
  • Q&A or feedback if needed

5. 🧭 Division of Roles & Responsibilities (15–20 min)

RoleResponsibilities
PresidentOversees the organization’s vision and operations, leads meetings, represents NRNA Belgium in official settings.
Vice President(s)Supports the President, steps in during their absence, coordinates with subcommittees or focus groups.
SecretaryMaintains meeting minutes, handles official communication and documentation, manages member lists.
TreasurerManages finances, prepares budgets and reports, ensures transparency and compliance with financial policies.
Communications OfficerOversees internal and external communication (email, social media, newsletters), ensures messaging aligns with the mission.
Events CoordinatorPlans and manages logistics for cultural, social, and community events.
Youth & Community OfficerEngages with the younger generation, proposes initiatives for integration, mentoring, and support.
IT/Tech Coordinator (optional)Manages digital tools (e.g., shared drives, online voting, website), ensures smooth communication channels.
General Members / VolunteersSupport various projects, assist executive members, bring in fresh ideas and local contacts.

6. 📅 Setting Meeting Preferences & Planning (10–15 min)

  • Discussion of meeting frequency
  • Best days/times for majority
  • Communication platforms to be used

7. 🗓️ Scheduling Upcoming Meetings (5 min)

  • Confirm next meeting date
  • Agree on a quarterly planning timeline

8. ✅ First Assignments & Setting Priorities (20–30 min)

  • Group brainstorm: Top 3 short-term goals
  • Assign responsibility to individuals or teams
  • Agree on next steps and internal deadlines

9. 🗣️ Open Discussion & Q&A (15 min)

  • Open floor for member input
  • Discussion of new ideas or community issues

10. 📌 Summary & Closing (5 min)

  • Recap key decisions and action points
  • Final remarks from Chairperson

11. ☕ Informal Networking (Optional)

  • Light snacks
  • Social interaction and community bonding
  • Introduction to ongoing or planned initiatives
